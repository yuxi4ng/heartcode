About us

What is HeartCode:

nitiative by Ellipsis, in collaboration with Devera to expose underprivileged youths to programming and computer science concepts.


Vision:

To create a sustainable framework for teaching underprivileged students, to be implemented nationwide


Mission:

That through our program, a passion for programming and problem solving will be ignited within these youths.

Social Issue

Animal Curelty
Cruelty to animals, also called animal abuse, animal neglect or simply animal cruelty, is the infliction by omission (animal neglect) or by commission by humans of suffering or harm upon any non-human animal, regardless of whether the act is against the law.

Racial Harmony

Racial Harmony Day is celebrated annually on 21 July in Singapore. The event is to commemorate the 1964 Race Riots, which took place on 21 July 1964.
Racial Harmony Day also represents a day for schools to reflect on, and celebrate Singapore's success as a racially harmonious nation and society built on a rich diversity of culture and heritage.

Littering

Litter can also be used as a verb. To litter means to drop and leave objects, often man-made, such as aluminum cans, cardboard boxes or plastic bottles on the ground and leave them there indefinitely or for others to dispose of as opposed to disposing of them properly.

Foreign Worker

A "Foreign worker" is a person who either migrates within their home country or outside it to pursue work such as seasonal work. Migrant workers usually do not have an intention to stay permanently in the country or region in which they work.
